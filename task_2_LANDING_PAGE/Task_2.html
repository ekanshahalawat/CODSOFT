Frequently Asked Questions

What is the battery life of the Smart Watch Pro?

The Smart Watch Pro offers up to 10 days of battery life with regular use and supports fast wireless charging.

Is the Smart Watch Pro waterproof?

Yes, it is IP68 waterproof, suitable for swimming up to 50 meters with a water lock mode for added protection.

Can I customize the watch face?

Absolutely! You can choose from thousands of downloadable watch faces to personalize your Smart Watch Pro.

Does it support notifications from my phone?

Yes, the Smart Watch Pro supports smart notifications for calls, texts, and app alerts directly on your wrist.
